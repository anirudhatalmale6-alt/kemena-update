Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026

Kemena Update ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Kemena Update mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our app.

Information Collection and Use

Kemena Update is a news reader application that fetches publicly available articles from kemenaupdate.com. We do not require user registration or login. The app collects minimal data to provide its services:

  • Device Token: We collect your device's push notification token to send you news alerts when new articles are published. This token is used solely for delivering notifications.
  • Bookmarks: Articles you bookmark are stored locally on your device only. We do not have access to your bookmarks.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Google AdMob: We display advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device information and use advertising identifiers to serve relevant ads. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more information.
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging: We use Firebase Cloud Messaging to send push notifications. Firebase may collect device identifiers and usage data. Please refer to Firebase Privacy Policy for details.
  • Google Translate: The app offers article translation using Google Translate. Article text is sent to Google's translation service when you use the translate feature. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.

Data Security

We value your trust and strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the Kemena Update website at kemenaupdate.com.
